TNUSRB Constable Notification 2022 - IAS Academy In Coimbatore

 

Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board (TNUSRB) has released an official notification announcing 3552 vacancies for group II Police Constable, Fireman, and Jail Warder posts. The eligible candidates will be shortlisted through Written Exam, Physical Measurement Test (PMT), Physical Endurance Test (PET), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The online application process will start on 7th July 2022 and end on 15th August 2022. For more information about the TNUSRB Constable Recruitment 2022, the candidates can refer to the details mentioned in the article.

TNUSRB Constable Recruitment 2022 Apply Online

 

The applications for TNUSRB Police Constable Recruitment 2022 have to be submitted through online mode only. The application window will remain active from 7th July 2022 to 15th August 2022 on the official website https://www.tnusrbonline.org/. The direct link to apply online for TNUSRB Constable Recruitment 2022 will be made available here as soon as the officials starts the process. 

 

TNUSRB Application Fee

 

After filling out the TNUSRB Recruitment Application Form, candidates need to pay the required application fee through credit or debit cards. The application fee is non-refundable, that is, cannot be redeemed back once paid.

Steps to Apply for TNUSRB Recruitment 2022

 

Step 1: Visit the TNUSRB official website at https://www.tnusrbonline.org/.

Step 2: Click on the “Careers/Notification” tab on the Homepage.

Step 3: Search for TNUSRB Police Constable Notification 2022 and click on it.

Step 4: Fill out the TNUSRB Police Constable 2022 application form with all the mandatory details.

Step 5: Upload all the required documents including your passport size photograph, signature, certificates, etc. You can use the Testbook Cropping Tool to upload Your Image & Signature in specific dimensions.

Step 6: Recheck all the details of your application form before proceeding to the payment page.

Step 7: Pay the application fees as mentioned category-wise.

Step 8: Click on the submit button.

Step 9: After successfully submitting the application form applicants may note their registration number and take a printout copy of the confirmation page

TNUSRB Constable Recruitment 2022 Eligibility 

Candidates must fulfil the TNUSRB Constable eligibility criteria listed below to appear for the TNUSRB Recruitment 2022. These Eligibility details in terms of age limit, qualification, etc mentioned below are as per the previous notification 2020. 

TNUSRB Police Constable Educational Qualification

The applicants must have passed class 10th with Tamil as one of the languages in the curriculum. Knowledge of the Tamil Language is mandatory.
 

 

TNUSRB Police Constable Age Limit  (As on 1st July 2022)

 

The age of the applicants must be between 18- 26 years. The age relaxations expected to be allowed are as mentioned below

 

 

BC and MBC

18 to 26 years

SC, ST, and SCA 

18 to 29 years

Female Widow

18 to 35 years

Ex-serviceman

Up to 45 years
